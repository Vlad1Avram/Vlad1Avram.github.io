About the festival


Welcome to StringFest, the heart and soul of musical celebration in Cluj-Napoca! Nestled in the cultural hub of Transylvania, StringFest is an annual festival that weaves together the enchanting melodies of old and contemporary acoustic music, creating a harmonious tapestry that resonates through the  streets of Cluj-Napoca. Join us from the 2nd of July to the 5th for an unforgettable journey into the world of music.

Schedule

02 July 2024

Warm-up chords 

This day is dedicated to getting in the vibe. Live acts start at noon, but as you have come to expect by now, festival gates are open at 12:00. Come, enjoy the ride!

03 July 2024

From morning to... morning

Full. On. Music. Non-stop, yes, this means 24 hours, of concerts, whether on the main stage or one of the other 3 stages you've come to expect from us: Chill Garden, Funky Tent, Dance grounds. 50 solo acts and bands. If you want to see them, they are here. You only have to check the timetable.

04 July 2024

Slowly, but surely...

Don't worry. You'll know when the festival ends because we're lighting up the sky at midnight with lanterns, limelight and a laser show. Our final act will be incendiary, with The Promenaders returning for the 3rd time to close off the best acoustic festival in Cluj-Napoca. But until midnight, it's music as usual!
